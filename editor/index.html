Instructions
  • Shift-click on the background to create a new node.
  • Alt-click on a node or link to delete it.
  • Click and drag a node while holding Ctrl to move it.
  • Click and drag one node to another to link them.
  • Click on a link and press T to toggle its direction.
  • Click on a node and press T to toggle its reflexivity.
  • Your work is automatically saved.
  • To save a preset, press S.
  • To start from scratch, press X.
  • To start over with the default graph, press D.
  • To copy the matrix code, press Ctrl-C.
